Crab Curry with Rice

Ingredients

  • 400g cleaned Crab
  • 20g corriander powder
  • 10g red chilli powder
  • 2 tbls oil
  • 1 cloves,cinnamon stick,cardamon
  • 1 tsp Fennel seeds,curry leaves
  • 1 tbs Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1 large onion,sliced
  • 1 Tomato
  • 300ml Coconut Milk
  • 200ml water

Directions

Warm the pan, add oil to the pan. when the oil is hot, toss the spices and afte a minute add the sliced onions

When the onions are well saute and are turning into a beutiful caramelized color , add the ginger garlic paste.after 2 minutes, add the corriander power, chilli powder and salt to taste.

add the crab and water, then simmer.once it boils for 13minutes,add coconut milk to the pan. After 4 minutes, remove the pan from heat. your curry is ready to be served with rice
